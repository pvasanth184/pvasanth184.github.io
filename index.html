About

Welcome to my webpage. I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in Mathematics at the University of Maryland, College Park, under the supervision of Prof. Yanir A. Rubinstein. I received my Bachelors and Masters in Mathematics from the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru, India, in 2021.

I am broadly interested in geometric analysis and non-linear PDE, with an emphasis on the special Lagrangian and degenerate special Lagrangian equations.
